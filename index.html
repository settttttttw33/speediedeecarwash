Frequently asked questions

What kind of pricing do you offer?

Speedie D's offers single washes, unlimited monthly fast passes, and custom fleet conversations based on your vehicle count and wash needs.

Can my business use the monthly plans?

Yes. Monthly fast passes are a simple fit for repeat vehicles. For several vehicles or prepaid wash needs, send us a note and we will help match the right setup.

Can I cancel a monthly fast pass?

The current Speedie D's offer is month-to-month with no contract. Use the contact form or pass portal for help managing a plan.

What vehicles can use the wash?

Vehicles must fit the 7'2" height maximum and 13" tire width maximum. Dually vehicles are not supported.
