Privacy policy.

This prototype stores account and form data only in your browser's local storage. It does not transmit payment, login, rewards, or contact information to Speedie D's or a third party.

Information collected

Account details, vehicle information, plan selections, rewards activity, and form entries are saved locally to demonstrate the requested website functionality.

SMS consent

SMS language is shown for interface completeness. This local prototype does not send text messages. Production use requires an approved messaging provider, consent records, opt-out handling, and the final business policy.

Payment information

No real payment is processed. The demo saves only the last four card digits entered during the simulated checkout.
